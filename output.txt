EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE (EI) INVOLVES RECOGNIZING, 
UNDERSTANDING, AND MANAGING EMOTIONS IN ONESELF 
AND OTHERS TO IMPROVE RELATIONSHIPS AND OUTCOMES. 
KEY COMPONENTS OFTEN CITED INCLUDE SELF-AWARENESS 
(KNOWING YOUR EMOTIONS), SELF-REGULATION (MANAGING EMOTIONS)
, MOTIVATION (DRIVING ACHIEVEMENT), EMPATHY 
(UNDERSTANDING OTHERS' FEELINGS), AND 
SOCIAL SKILLS (MANAGING RELATIONSHIPS).
